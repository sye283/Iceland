ICELAND ITINERARY

DAY 1:

  1. 3:30pm - 7:30pm
  2. Reykjanes peninsula
    • Ink-black lava field
    • BLUE LAGOON!! (with silica mud mask?!)
      • 37-39 C, 99-102 F
      • formed 1976 in conjunction with nearby geothermal power plant
      • BRING: swimwear, towel
      • OTHER: storage cost = 3 EUR (500 ISK) per bag
    • Recommended: LAVA restaurant (probably an expensive rip-off)
  3. 8:30pm - 11:30pm
  4. Northern Lights Minibus tour
    • BRING: sturdy shoes, warm clothing
    • OTHER: hot chocolate and ginger biscuits included

DAY 2: (All part of Golden Circle)

  1. 8:00am - 6:00pm
  2. HORSEBACK RIDING!!
  3. Bingvellir National Park
    • Almannagja: Forge that marks the eastern boundary of North American plate
    • Oxararfoss waterfall (GAME OF THRONES FILMED HERE !!)
    • Bingvallavatn: largest natural lake in Iceland
      • unique snorkeling/scuba diving experience
    • Gjabakkarhellir: lava tube formed from eruption 9000 years ago (pleasant walk)
  4. Haukadalur Valley
    • "The Great Geyser"
      • Not very active
      • Eruption: 70m (230ft)
    • Strokkur
      • Erupts every 10 mins
      • Eruption: 20m (65ft) to 40m (130ft)
      • Fumaroles: vent for steam and hot gasses in Earth's crust
        • Yellow stains = native sulfur (crystallizes from steam)
        • Mud pots = fumaroles that boil up through surface water/ground water
      • Gulgoss (Golden) Waterfall
        • 32m (104ft)
      • BRING: Sturdy shoes, gloves, warm clothing
      • OTHER: Lunch at Hotel Eldhestar

DAY 3:

  1. 9:00am (be ready at 8:15am)
  2. Seljalandsfoss waterfall
    • 63m (207ft)
  3. Skogafoss waterfall
    • 60m (200ft)
  4. Reynisfjara: black sand beach
  5. LUNCH BREAK (I think in Vik?)
  6. Black sand plains (result of volcanic eruption) and Vatnajokull glacier
  7. Jokulsarlon glacier lagoon
    • deep blue color of underside = oxygen bubbles compressed into ice for thousands of years
    • might see seals on ice (more likely next day when we visit midday)
    • Batman Begins was filmed here !!
  8. Northern lights hunting if clear skies
  9. BRING: warm clothing, hiking shoes, camera
  10. OTHER: Breakfast included at Hotel Hofn

DAY 4:

  1. Blue ice cave by Vatnajokull National Park
    • Vatnajokull = Iceland's largest glacier
    • Hvannadalshnjukur = Iceland's tallest mountain
    • Caves might be flooding at this time of year --> unsafe
  2. Jokulsarlon glacier lagoon (another stop because it looks different in midday)
  3. BRING: warm clothing, hiking shoes, camera
  4. OTHER: breakfast and wifi included

DAY 5: LAST DAY

  1. Airport transfer to Keflavik airport (45 mins shuttle ride)
  2. Shuttle pick-up time: 2:00pm
